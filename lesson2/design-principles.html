PARC: Contrast

Rolex

https://www.rolex.com/es

This page shows a good contrast because the watch in the background has a semi-transparent layer that makes the white font more visible and readable even when the watch is on movement. All the attention is centered on the words and they have a hierarchical composition.

Hick's Law

Tesla

https://www.tesla.com/

Tesla's webpage exemplified Hick's Law because the options and complexity of the page are minimal, for the user is simpler to understand and make decisions in less time. On this page, whose purpose is to sell cars, giving the user an uncomplicated layout is a good way to help them to decide.

White Space

Cine Colombia

https://www.cinecolombia.com/ibague

This site is a proper example of white space because this page has a lot of options and is necessary to use the white space to separate the elements (information, buttons, etc.) in groups to facilitate the user experience and give visual rest spaces. The padding helps to organize the content to be more visible and understandable.
