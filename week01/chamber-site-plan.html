Ocobos' Chamber of Commerce

Site Plan

Site Name

The name of the site is Ocobos' Chamber of Commerce. The selected available domain is ocoboschamber.com. The word Ocobos relates the page of the Cahmber of Commerce with the City of Ibagué, where the ocobo tree is representative and also gives the idea of eco-friendly focus.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to invite more eco-friendly bussinesses in the city of Ibagué to join the Ocobos Chamber of Commerce, give information to the community's members, and show the results of the labor that we are doing to offer our products and services being kind with nature.

Scenarios

1. What can be considered an eco-firendly bussiness?
2. Why is important to offer sustainable products and services?
3. How can I contribute from my bussiness to a cleaner environment?
4. What are some ideas of eco-friendly practices that all bussiness can implement?
5. How my bussiness can be in agreement with the local and national goverment policies?
